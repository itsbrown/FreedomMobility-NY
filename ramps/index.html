Frequently Asked Questions About Ramps

How much do home ramps cost in Rochester, Buffalo or Syracuse?

Prices vary by type (portable vs permanent), length, and materials. Portable ramps often start under $1,000 while custom permanent ramps range $2,000–$6,000 installed. Free quotes available.

Are portable ramps a good option for temporary use?

Yes. Our portable folding ramps are ideal for short-term needs or rentals in the Rochester, Buffalo and Syracuse areas and can be stored easily when not in use.

Do you install ramps for businesses and churches too?

Absolutely. We provide commercial and institutional ramp solutions that meet accessibility codes for businesses, places of worship and public buildings throughout Western and Central New York.
