Frequently Asked Questions About Stairlifts

How much does a stairlift cost in Rochester, Buffalo, or Syracuse?

Pricing varies based on the model (straight vs curved), length of the staircase, and any custom features. Most straight stairlifts start around $3,000–$5,000 installed. We provide free in-home quotes with no obligation.

Can stairlifts be installed on curved stairs?

Yes. We specialize in custom curved stairlifts that fit the unique shape of your staircase in older Rochester, Buffalo, and Syracuse homes.

How long does installation take?

Most straight stairlifts can be installed in a few hours. Curved models typically take 1–2 days. Our technicians are clean and respectful of your home.
