WHO WE ARE

About Freedom Mobility NY

At Freedom Mobility NY, we believe everyone deserves the freedom to move comfortably and safely in their own home. Based in Rochester, we serve the greater Rochester, Buffalo, and Syracuse areas, helping individuals with mobility challenges regain independence through expertly designed accessibility solutions.

Our team of dedicated professionals is passionate about making a difference, offering personalized service from free in-home consultations to professional installation and ongoing support.

Customer-First Approach

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen first, then deliver solutions that truly fit your life and home.
