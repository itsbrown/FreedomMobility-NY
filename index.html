At Freedom Mobility NY, we believe everyone deserves the freedom to move comfortably and safely in their own home. Based in Rochester, we proudly serve the greater Rochester, Buffalo, and Syracuse areas.

Freedom Mobility is a leading provider of stairlifts, ramps, platform lifts, home modifications, bathroom safety, and more. We start with a free in-home consultation so we can learn about your needs. Our experts assess areas of concern, take measurements, and fully understand your mobility goals. As a locally owned company, you can rest easy knowing we are only a phone call away.
