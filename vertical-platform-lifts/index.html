Vertical Platform Lifts

Our vertical platform lifts cater to the needs of individuals using wheelchairs and power chairs, granting them access to raised porches, decks, and other elevated areas in both residential and commercial settings.

Designed for safety and reliability, our lifts are easy to operate and built to withstand the elements. These mobility solutions, resembling small elevators, smoothly transport users vertically.

Frequently Asked Questions About Vertical Platform Lifts

How much does a vertical platform lift cost in Rochester, Buffalo or Syracuse?

VPL pricing depends on lift height, capacity, and whether it's indoor or outdoor. Most residential units range from $8,000 to $15,000 installed. We offer free site evaluations.

Can vertical lifts be used outdoors in New York winters?

Yes. Our outdoor vertical platform lifts are weather-resistant and built to handle snow, ice and temperature swings common in Rochester, Buffalo and Syracuse.

Are vertical platform lifts safe for wheelchair users?

Absolutely. All our VPLs include safety gates, non-skid platforms, emergency stops and meet applicable safety standards for reliable wheelchair and power chair access.
